# Triple Space Translator Release Copy

![Release Cover](../assets/marketing/release-cover.png)

## Headline

Triple-space your way from Chinese to English.

## Short Intro

Triple Space Translator lets you type in Chinese or English, press `Space` three times within `0.5s`, and replace the current text in place. Press triple-space again to toggle back.

## Why It Matters

- Keep your natural-language thinking flow.
- Switch to English only when you need better output from global AI tools.
- Stay inside the same chat box, search field, or editor.
- Skip copy-paste and translation-site detours.

## Current Platform Status

- macOS: ready, using Apple `Translation.framework`
- Windows: stable build supports online API translation
- Windows offline bundled mode: still being completed

## Good Scenarios

- ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Gemini
- Browser search bars
- Messages and quick replies
- Prompt writing and short drafting
